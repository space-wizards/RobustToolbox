Class StyleBoxTexture

Style box based on a 9-patch texture. An image is divided into up to nine regions by splitting the image along each PatchMargin. The corner pieces will be drawn once, at their original size, while the Mode controls the (up to five) central pieces which can be either stretched or tiled to fill up the space the box is being drawn in.
